Into the Green Green Mud

February 27, 2012 in greenmud, novel, soundtrack.

We’re teaming up with OddBird to publish Into the Green Green Mud, a new multimedia novel written & illustrated by Eric. We’re also creating a soundtrack, which may grow into a full performance.

Eric is working hard to put it all online, and the process is public, so feel free to follow along.
